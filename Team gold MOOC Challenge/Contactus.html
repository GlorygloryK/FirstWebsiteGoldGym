FAQs

What are the prices of your membership

  • We have Membership packages starting from £16.99 to £20 per month
  • Details for packages can be sent to your email after filling in the contact form

How do you book gym classes?

  • Once you gain a membership you'd be able to download our GoldGym App, register and see what classes we have available

How do you pay for membership?

  • Both cash and card are accepted to pay for membership
  • You can pay by card link sent to your email once you fill in the contact form and either by card or physically at our gym locations

How do you get priced for membership?

  • Usually every month , from the same day you paid your first bill
  • E.g. if you paid your first month on the 5th November, your next due date would be the 6th December for renewal of gym payment
    Payment can be done via debit card or paid monthly by the person themselves
